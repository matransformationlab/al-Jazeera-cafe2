Our Story

From Somali-Kenyan family kitchen to Bungoma's table

Founded by Omar Sharif in the early 2010s, Al Jazeera Cafe began as a Somali-Kenyan family kitchen with one mission: feed people honest food and treat every guest like family.

Our first menu was simple but legendary: pilau, meat stew, and ugali na nyama. Over the years, we've become Bungoma's meeting point ‚Äî for students grabbing lunch, families celebrating milestones, travelers needing a clean bed, and friends gathering to watch football on Friday nights.

What makes us different? We're not just a restaurant ‚Äî we're a community hub where Kenyans and Somalis come together. Al Jazeera is a legend and part of Bungoma's history.
